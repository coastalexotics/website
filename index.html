The Club

Built for the drive — and the people who live for it.

Coastal Exotics began with a simple idea: bring together California's most passionate supercar and hypercar owners for the experiences that make ownership unforgettable. From the cliffs of Big Sur to the canyons inland, we turn extraordinary cars into extraordinary days.

Headquartered in Carmel-by-the-Sea with members from San Diego to the Bay Area, we're a community first — united by craftsmanship, horsepower, and the open road.

  • Curated rallies on California's most legendary routes.
  • Members-only events and private gatherings.
  • Priority passes to outside automotive events.

Membership

More than a club. A way to drive.

Every membership is your access to the experiences, the routes, and the people that make Coastal Exotics what it is.

Car Rallies

Signature group drives along Highway 1, the Carmel Valley, and beyond — fuel stops, photo ops, and unforgettable roads, all expertly planned.

Exclusive Events

Private dinners, track days, cars & coffee mornings and seasonal showcases — curated gatherings reserved for members and their guests.

Outside Event Passes

Priority access and passes to premier automotive events across California — concours, auctions, motorsport weekends and partner experiences.

Charity Drives

Give back through charity rallies and fundraising events — bringing the community together for causes that matter, on the roads we love.

The Community

A vetted network of owners who share your passion — friendships, advice, and the kind of camaraderie that only the right people bring.

Concierge Touches

From routing and reservations to detailing partners and storage, membership comes with the details handled — so you can focus on the drive.
